The LSI is a calculated number that predicts whether pool water will tend to corrode or scale. PoolFlow calculates LSI in real-time with CYA correction, factoring in pH, temperature, calcium hardness, total alkalinity, TDS, and cyanuric acid. An LSI between -0.3 and +0.3 is balanced.
